Become an Examiner for Judur Charity

As an examiner at Judur Charity, you will play a vital role in helping us ensure that the land donated by kind-hearted individuals is suitable for our food festivals. Your job will be to visit the land, examine its condition, and report back to the admin with your findings. The report you provide will be used to make key decisions on whether the land is ready for the festival.

Judur Charity is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping people in need. Your contribution as an examiner will be highly valued and will help us make a positive impact in our community.

We thank you for your commitment and look forward to working with you to bring about real change.
